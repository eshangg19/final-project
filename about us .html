About Us

KUSINA VALENTIN

Kusina Valentin is a warm and welcoming restaurant dedicated to serving flavorful, home-style dishes that celebrate the rich traditions of Filipino cuisine. Founded with a passion for authentic cooking and heartfelt hospitality, our kitchen is inspired by the comfort of family gatherings and the joy of sharing good food with the people you love.

At Kusina Valentin, every meal is prepared with care, using fresh ingredients and recipes passed down through generations. Whether you’re here for a quick bite, a family celebration, or simply to enjoy your favorite Filipino classics, we aim to make every visit feel like coming home.

Located near the iconic Paoay Church, you can also enjoy the stunning view of this historic landmark while dining—adding a unique and memorable touch to your experience.
